About Me

Who I Am

Hi, I'm Reden — a 22-year-old 4th Year BSIT student at STI College Lipa. I'm passionate about front-end development, UI/UX design, and visual storytelling — constantly finding ways to blend code with creativity.

Born on June 1, 2003 in Lipa City, Batangas, I've always been drawn to the intersection of technology and design. As Technical Director, Lead Photographer, Social Media Manager, and Graphic Designer of Ocularis — STI Lipa's official media team — I've built a body of work that reflects both technical precision and artistic vision.

My journey is about continuous learning: pushing the boundaries of what's possible when passion meets purpose.
